Pronunciation Guide

Improve your speaking with simple sound tips.

1. The “TH” Sound

Place your tongue lightly between your teeth and push air out.
Examples: think, through, theory

2. Ending Sounds

Pronounce the last letter clearly (t, d, k).
Example: "fast", "cold", "speak"

3. Stress on Syllables

Say one part of the word louder.
Example: PRO-gress (noun), pro-GRESS (verb)
