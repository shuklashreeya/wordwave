Grammar Basics

Understand English grammar with simple explanations.

1. Parts of Speech

Noun: Name of a person, place, or thing.

Example: The cat is sleeping.

2. Tenses

Present: I eat.

Past: I ate.

Future: I will eat.

3. Sentence Structure

Rule: Subject + Verb + Object

Example: She (subject) reads (verb) books (object).
